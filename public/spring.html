Spring Packages

Arunachal Pradesh

Experience Arunachal Pradesh's breathtaking landscapes, rich tribal heritage, and serene monasteries.

  • Duration: 7-10 days
  • Highlights: Tawang Monastery, Talley Valley Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Activities: Trekking, nature walks, cultural tours
Andaman and Nicobar Islands

Enjoy the pristine beaches, clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

  • Duration: 6-8 days
  • Highlights: Cellular Jail, Radhanagar Beach, Bharatpur Beach
  • Activities: Scuba diving, snorkeling, beach activities
Karnataka

Discover Karnataka's diverse attractions, from Bangalore's tech hubs to Hampi's ancient ruins and Coorg's lush hills.

  • Duration: 7-10 days
  • Highlights: Hampi ruins, Mysore Palace, Coorg coffee plantations
  • Activities: Heritage tours, wildlife safaris, coffee estate visits
Goa

Enjoy the sun-kissed beaches, vibrant nightlife, and diverse culture of Goa, India's premier coastal destination.

  • Duration: 4-7 days
  • Highlights: Baga Beach, Anjuna Beach, Dudhsagar Falls
  • Activities: Water sports, nightlife, heritage tours
Kerala

Immerse yourself in the lush backwaters, serene beaches, and rich traditions of Kerala, "God's Own Country."

  • Duration: 6-9 days
  • Highlights: Backwaters, Tea Gardens, Vembanad Lake
  • Activities: Houseboat cruise, nature walks, spice plantation tours
Rajasthan

Discover the royal palaces, vibrant culture, and expansive deserts of Rajasthan, the land of kings.

  • Duration: 7-10 days
  • Highlights: City Palace, Lake Pichola, Mehrangarh Fort
  • Activities: Camel rides, folk dance performances, palace visits
