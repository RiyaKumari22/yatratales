Explore the Golden Triangle: Delhi's heritage, Agra's Taj Mahal, and Jaipur's palaces.

  • Activities: Sightseeing, shopping, cultural tours
Sikkim

Discover Sikkim's stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and majestic Himalayan views.

  • Duration: 6-9 days
  • Highlights: Tsomgo Lake, Kanchenjunga Falls, Rumtek Monastery
  • Activities: Trekking, sightseeing, cultural tours
Ladakh

Embark on an adventurous journey through the rugged landscapes, high-altitude deserts, and serene monasteries of Leh-Ladakh.

  • Duration: 8-12 days
  • Highlights: Pangong Lake, Nubra Valley, Magnetic Hill

Goa

Enjoy the sun-kissed beaches, vibrant nightlife, and diverse culture of Goa, India's premier coastal destination.

  • Duration: 4-7 days
  • Highlights: Baga Beach, Anjuna Beach, Dudhsagar Falls
  • Activities: Water sports, nightlife, heritage tours
Kerala

Immerse yourself in the lush backwaters, serene beaches, and rich traditions of Kerala, "God's Own Country."

  • Duration: 6-9 days
  • Highlights: Backwaters, Tea Gardens, Vembanad Lake
  • Activities: Houseboat cruise, nature walks, spice plantation tours
Rajasthan

Discover the royal palaces, vibrant culture, and expansive deserts of Rajasthan, the land of kings.

  • Duration: 7-10 days
  • Highlights: City Palace, Lake Pichola, Mehrangarh Fort
  • Activities: Camel rides, folk dance performances, palace visits
Tamil Nadu

Explore the ancient temples, cultural heritage, and scenic landscapes of Tamil Nadu, a southern gem of India.

  • Duration: 6-8 days
  • Highlights: Marina Beach, Shore Temple, Meenakshi Temple
  • Activities: Temple tours, cultural performances, beach outings
Madhya Pradesh

Uncover the rich history, diverse wildlife, and architectural marvels of Madhya Pradesh, the heart of India.

  • Duration: 7-10 days
  • Highlights: Wildlife viewing, Bandhavgarh Fort, Jungle Safaris
  • Activities: Tiger safaris, nature walks, bird watching
