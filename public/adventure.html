Adventure: Discover the thrilling experience

Trekking and Hiking

Himalayas: The Ultimate Trekking Destination

The Himalayas are a trekker's paradise. Famous trails like the Roopkund Trek, Chadar Trek, and Markha Valley Trek offer stunning views and challenging routes. Whether you're a seasoned trekker or a beginner, the Himalayas have something for everyone.

Paragliding

Soar High in Bir Billing

Bir Billing in Himachal Pradesh is one of the best paragliding spots in the world. Experience the thrill of flying over lush green valleys and snow-capped peaks. The annual Paragliding World Cup held here attracts participants from around the globe.

River Rafting

Conquer the Rapids of the Ganges

Rishikesh, known as the 'Yoga Capital of the World,' is also a hotspot for river rafting. The Ganges River offers thrilling rapids ranging from Grade I to Grade IV, providing an exhilarating experience for both novices and experts.If you are confused about when to go for rafting in Rishikesh, we are going to provide you complete assistance on this. In second week of September, rafting starts in Rishikesh and continues till June with complete fun and thrill.

Scuba Diving

Dive into the Azure Waters of the Andamans

The Andaman and Nicobar Islands offer crystal clear waters and vibrant coral reefs, making it a perfect spot for scuba diving. Explore the underwater world teeming with marine life, including colorful fish, turtles, and even sharks.

Wildlife Safaris

Explore the Wilderness in Ranthambore

Ranthambore National Park in Rajasthan is famous for its tiger population. Embark on a wildlife safari to spot tigers, leopards, and a variety of other wildlife in their natural habitat. The park also boasts ancient ruins, adding a touch of history to your adventure.

Desert Safari

Experience the Thar Desert in Jaisalmer

Jaisalmer, known as the 'Golden City,' offers an authentic desert safari experience. Ride camels across the golden sands, witness breathtaking sunsets, and enjoy cultural performances under the starlit sky.

Rock Climbing

Scale the Rocks in Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Karnataka, is also a renowned destination for rock climbing. The unique granite boulders provide a perfect playground for climbers of all levels. The surreal landscape adds to the charm of the climbing experience..

Bungee Jumping

Take the Leap in Rishikesh

Rishikesh is not only famous for river rafting but also for bungee jumping. Jumpin Heights, located in Mohan Chatti, offers one of the highest bungee jumping experiences in India. Feel the adrenaline rush as you leap off a 83-meter high platform.

Skiing

Hit the Slopes in Auli

Auli in Uttarakhand is a popular skiing destination in India. Known for its well-groomed slopes and stunning views of the surrounding Himalayan peaks, Auli offers excellent skiing conditions for beginners and seasoned skiers alike.

Hot Air Ballooning

Float Above the Pink City, Jaipur

Experience the magic of floating above Jaipur, the 'Pink City,' in a hot air balloon. Soar over forts, palaces, and the beautiful Aravalli ranges. This unique adventure provides a bird’s-eye view of the city’s rich heritage and landscape.

Zip Lining

Glide Over Forts in Neemrana

Neemrana Fort in Rajasthan offers a thrilling zip-lining experience. Glide over the ancient fort and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. This activity combines adventure with a touch of history.
